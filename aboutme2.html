MUSIC

What is music?
Music is the vocal or instrumental sounds (or both) combined in such a way as to produce beauty of form, harmony, and expression of emotion. Everything you hear around you is a form of music. But, don’t mistake noise for music. Music is meant to sound good, so when you combine different noises together to form sounds that are pleasing to the ear, you’ve created music!

There are many genres of music. A few examples are as follows:

Pop
Rock
Jazz
R&B
World
Musical Theatre
Folk Music
And many more.

POP MUSIC Pop music is the genre of popular music that produces the most hits. A hit is a song that sells many copies, and the latest hits are listed every week on the charts. They have a good rhythm, a catchy melody, and are easy to remember and sing along to. Pop songs are not necessarily *produced* by pop artists. Artists who sing R&B, soul music, country music, and rappers are also pop music artists. Ariana Grande, Pharell Williams, Katy Perry, Adele, Khalid, and Eminem, Whitney Houston are a few famous pop music artists. Listen to a few of their hit songs, and you’ll know what I mean! You’ll love them.

*FUN FACT! : Michael Jackson and Madonna are respectively known as the King and Queen of Pop.
